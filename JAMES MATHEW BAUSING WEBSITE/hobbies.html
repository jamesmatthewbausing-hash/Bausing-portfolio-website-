Classroom of
the Elite

My all-time favorite anime is Classroom of the Elite (ようこそ実力至上主義の教室へ). It follows Kiyotaka Ayanokoji, a student who deliberately hides his genius intellect to observe the social dynamics of his hyper-competitive school.

What draws me to this anime is the psychological depth, strategic thinking, and the idea that true intelligence isn't always loud. It resonates with my own belief in working smart and learning continuously.

  • Studio: Lerche
  • Genre: Drama · Psychological · School
  • Seasons: 3 (+ OVAs)
  • Why I love it: Mind games & strategic storytelling
